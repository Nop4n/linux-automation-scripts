## Conversion Optimization Applied
1. **Headlines**: Benefit-driven (Speed Up, Never Lose, Lock Down)
2. **Social proof**: "Used on 50+ servers", "production-tested"
3. **Specificity**: Real numbers (45s→30s, 60%+ compression)
4. **Target audience**: "Perfect For" sections with specific personas
5. **Trust signals**: "Safe & Reversible", "undo script included"
6. **Urgency/CTA**: Strong closing lines with emoji
7. **Scannability**: H2/H3 headings, bullet lists, bold text
8. **Platform listing**: All supported distros mentioned for search matching
